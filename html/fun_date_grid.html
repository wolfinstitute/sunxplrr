fun_date_grid {sunxplrr}R Documentation

Provides a tibble with dates from start to end

Description

Privides a tibble with dates and columns containing year, month and day. The granularity can be chosen from year month or day. Works for all years within the gregorian calendar.

Usage

fun_date_grid(start_date, end_date, granularity = "day")

Arguments

start_date

First date of grid.

end_date

Last date of grid.

granularity

Implemented are "year", "month" and "day".

Value

tibble containing date, and depending on the granularity also year, month and day.
